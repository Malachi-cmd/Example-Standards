
Copilot Instructions

This repository contains engineering and manufacturing standards.

## Requirements

- Read all standard documents before making recommendations.
- Prefer information from the EngrStd documents over assumptions.
- If a standard conflicts with a general best practice, follow the documented standard.
- Cite the source document when referencing a requirement.
- Do not invent requirements that are not present in the standards.

## Response Style

- Be concise.
- Use bullet points when summarizing standards.
- Clearly distinguish between documented requirements and suggestions.
